Concept Feature Extraction

Pass 50 zebra + 50 horse images through CLIP → SAE. Compute mean activation per hidden dim. Top-K dims where (zebra_mean − horse_mean) is largest = "zebra concept features". Output: concept_features.json

Challenges Encountered

Matryoshka SAE Incompatibility — Planned to use a pretrained Matryoshka SAE from a recent paper, but the compression layer it was trained on does not exist in LLaVA-1.5-7B's CLIP encoder. Had to train from scratch.
OOM Errors — Kaggle GPU P100 (16 GB) limited us to only ~10K images for feature extraction. Ideal training would use 100K+ images.
Low Expansion Ratio — Only 4× (1024 → 4096) expansion. State-of-art SAEs use 16×–64× for good monosemanticity. Our features remain polysemantic.
Only 2 Epochs — Insufficient training. SAE features are not yet well-separated, leading to poor concept isolation.
Result — concept_features.json produced, but the identified features are noisy. Downstream unlearning accuracy will be limited until SAE is improved.
