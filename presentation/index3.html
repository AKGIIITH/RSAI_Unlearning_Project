Literature Review

Key Papers & Foundations

SAE-Based Unlearning

Pach et al.

Unlearning Concepts from LLMs with SAE Feature-Level Interventions
Pioneered using SAE latent dimensions to selectively erase concepts from language models via feature clamping — our primary inspiration.
NeurIPS 2025

Farrell et al.

Applying Sparse Autoencoders to Unlearn Knowledge in Language Models
Extended SAE-based unlearning with comparative analysis of zero-ablation vs. negative steering vs. weight projection approaches.
arXiv 2024

Joseph et al.

Sparse Autoencoders for LLM Unlearning
Systematic study of SAE feature identification for concept removal; introduced differential activation method for concept feature selection.
arXiv 2024

MultiDelete — Li et al.

Multimodal Machine Unlearning
First benchmark for multimodal unlearning; defined forget/retain evaluation protocols for VLMs that we adapt for our pipeline.
ECCV 2024
Models & Benchmarks

Liu et al. — LLaVA

Visual Instruction Tuning (EvolvingLMMs Lab)
The VLM we target — LLaVA-1.5-7B connects CLIP ViT-L/14 vision encoder to Vicuna-7B LLM via a projection layer.
NeurIPS 2023

MUSE Benchmark

Machine Unlearning Six-Way Evaluation
Defines 6 metrics for unlearning evaluation: FA, RA, GU, LL, MIA, AR — we adopt FA, RA, LL, AR for our evaluation framework.
arXiv 2024
Inspiration Papers

Zaigrajew — Matryoshka SAEs

Multi-scale SAE architecture; attempted to use for our CLIP encoder but compression layer incompatible with LLaVA-1.5.
ICML 2025

SAEs in Vision Models

Demonstrated SAEs can decompose CLIP vision features into interpretable monosemantic directions — basis for training our custom SAE.
2024

Steering in CLIP

Showed SAE feature steering in CLIP can control visual concept activation — informs our multi-method steering strategy.
2024
