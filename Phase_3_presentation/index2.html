What Changed After the Proposal Review

Feedback Received

"Talk to Arvindh Arun (Precog alumni) about the project."
"It is not sure how practical the idea is. Need some pilot experimentation …"
The original proposal relied on circuit-level analysis — identifying and ablating specific attention heads / MLPs responsible for a concept. Mentors questioned the feasibility and scalability of this approach for multimodal models.

Major Pivot: Circuits → SAE

Circuit-based Ablation SAE-based Feature Clamping

Replaced circuit discovery with Sparse Autoencoder (SAE) training on CLIP vision encoder features, then identifying concept-specific latent dimensions for selective clamping during inference.

Pilot Experimentation Done

Trained a custom SAE on CLIP, built a full baseline evaluation pipeline, and created Forget/Retain datasets — demonstrating practical feasibility of the approach.
