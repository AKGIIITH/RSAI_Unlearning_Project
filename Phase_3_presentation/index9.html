Key Observations

FA is very high (98.67%) — The model has strong zebra recognition, giving us a clear signal to measure unlearning against.
RA is moderate (44.33%) — The 4-bit quantized model struggles with horse/donkey fine-grained recognition. Strict string matching contributes to the lower score.
LL is extremely high (96%) — Even without images, text decoder leaks zebra knowledge. This suggests the concept lives in both vision and language pathways.
AR at 50% — Adversarial image transforms (blur/grey) reduce recognition by ~half, showing visual feature degradation under perturbation.
